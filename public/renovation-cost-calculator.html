Renovation Budget FAQ

Singapore renovation cost questions

These answers help homeowners compare HDB, condo, resale, BTO, kitchen and toilet renovation estimates before speaking to a contractor.

How accurate is this renovation calculator?

It is a planning estimate based on selected scope, property type, size and common Singapore renovation cost items. Final pricing still depends on site measurements, hacking condition, material selection and approval requirements.

Can it estimate HDB and BTO renovation costs?

Yes. The calculator includes HDB flat types, BTO/new condition and resale condition so homeowners can compare broad budget ranges for common HDB renovation scopes.

Can I use it for kitchen or toilet renovation only?

Yes. Choose kitchen-only or toilet-only at the first step. The estimate will focus on relevant items such as carpentry, worktop, tiling, plumbing, sanitary fittings and shower screens.

Why does resale renovation usually cost more?

Resale homes often involve hacking, haulage, removal, making-good works and unknown site conditions. These can increase the final renovation cost after inspection.

Does the estimate include GST?

The calculator includes a GST toggle at the estimate step. Switch it on to view a GST-inclusive planning range.

How do I get a confirmed quotation?

Send the estimate to ID Work Studio on WhatsApp. A confirmed quotation requires floor plan review, site measurements, material confirmation and final scope checking.
